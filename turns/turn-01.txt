One important Apple Silicon consideration

Your Mac may be Apple Silicon, while your Hetzner VPS may be amd64. Codex should check the VPS architecture:

uname -m

Typical results:

x86_64   = linux/amd64
aarch64  = linux/arm64

OrbStack can run both ARM and Intel Linux images on Apple Silicon, including linux/amd64 images through emulation.

Codex should either build multi-platform images:

    platform: linux/amd64

The final architecture should not assume the Mac and VPS have the same processor architecture.
